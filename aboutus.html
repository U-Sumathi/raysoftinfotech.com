ABOUT US

Our Story

All of this started when one man had the vision to start his own business and to provide steady employment to young people. His vision became a reality in the year 2000.

Mr. Raju Sambandam kicked off his business journey from a small apartment with meager technical support and a couple of young minds.

Now our thriving business of 110+ employees and with a clientele globally, is still on the search for more.
